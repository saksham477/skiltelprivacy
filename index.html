3. How we use your face photos specifically

Because photos of your face are sensitive, here is a complete, specific explanation of what happens to them:

What is collected

The photo you take or select for a scan, downscaled on your device before upload to reduce size.

Why it's collected

Solely to generate your skin analysis: concern scores (hydration, pores, wrinkles, dark spots, redness, oiliness, acne), your overall skin score, a written narrative summary, and a personalized routine built from those results. Your photo is also used for basic photo-quality and face-detection checks (e.g. rejecting a photo that is too dark, too blurry, contains no face, or contains more than one face) before analysis runs.

Who it's shared with

Your photo is sent, over an encrypted connection, to YouCam (Perfect Corp), a third-party skin-analysis API provider, for the sole purpose of generating the concern scores described above. YouCam processes the image to return analysis results and does not receive your name, email, or any other account information alongside the photo. We do not share your face photos with advertisers, data brokers, or any party other than YouCam for this analysis purpose.

Where it's stored

After analysis, your original photo is stored in a private, access-controlled cloud storage bucket (hosted on Supabase) associated only with your account. It is not publicly accessible; only your authenticated account can retrieve it (via short-lived signed URLs).

How long it's retained

Your photos and the scan results derived from them are retained for as long as your account remains active, so you can review past scans and track progress over time. You may delete an individual scan or permanently delete your entire account (which immediately and permanently deletes all stored photos, scan results, and account data) at any time from Profile → Delete account in the App. Deleted data is removed from our storage and database as part of that action and cannot be recovered.

5. Tracking & advertising

SKINTEL does not track you. We do not use your data to serve targeted advertising, we do not combine your data with data from other companies for advertising purposes, and we do not share your data with data brokers. The App does not use the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), third-party advertising SDKs, or cross-app/cross-website tracking technologies. Because SKINTEL does not track users as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, the App does not display an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

6. Your choices & rights

  • Access: All your scan history, scores, and routines are viewable in-app at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete an individual scan, or permanently delete your entire account and all associated data, from Profile → Delete account.
  • Correction: You can update your onboarding profile (skin type, goals, concerns, etc.) at any time in the App.
  • Portability/inquiries: Contact us using the details below for any data export or privacy-related request, including requests available to you under GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, or other applicable privacy laws.

7. Security

Data is transmitted using encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. Photos and account data are stored in access-controlled cloud infrastructure (Supabase) scoped to your authenticated account. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable, industry-standard measures to protect your information.

8. Children's privacy

SKINTEL is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information, including face photos, from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.
